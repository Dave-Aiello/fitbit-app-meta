Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the OpenClaw Fitbit App (the "App") handles data.

1. Scope

The App is a personal, owner-operated integration used to retrieve Fitbit account data for personal productivity and health tracking workflows.

2. Data Collected

With your permission via Fitbit OAuth, the App may access Fitbit data such as profile, activity, sleep, heart rate, and weight data, depending on granted scopes.

3. How Data Is Used

Data is used only to display and analyze personal Fitbit information for the authorized account holder. Data is not sold and is not used for advertising.

4. Data Sharing

Data is not shared with third parties except as required for the App to function with Fitbit's API, or if required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens and related configuration are stored locally on the owner’s system with restricted file permissions. Reasonable safeguards are used to protect access.

6. Retention and Deletion

You may revoke app access at any time in Fitbit settings. Locally stored tokens can be removed by signing out of the CLI integration.

7. Third-Party Services

The App depends on Fitbit’s APIs and services, which are governed by Fitbit’s own terms and privacy policy.
